Try to break all breakable bricks on every level in this really great brick breaking game!

Detailed rules are in the game.

Features:
- Completely free without ads
- 4 Game Modes
- 190 levels
- 3 Game Speeds
- 21 different Power-Ups
- Two control modes (touch & tilt)
